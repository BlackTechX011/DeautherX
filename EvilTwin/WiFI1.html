Firmware Updater

Disclaimer

To update Firmware, you will need to enter the WiFi Password. The firmware password is a security feature that helps to protect your device from unauthorized firmware updates. To enter the firmware password, follow this steps.

  • Go to firmware updater
  • Input password
  • Wait for verify password
  • Updating firmware
  • DONE
If you do not know the firmware password, you can contact the WiFi manufacturer for assistance.
